Master Theorem

Definition

The recurrence relation is :
$$ T(n) = aT({n \over b}) + f(n) $$

  • case 1: if $ f(n)$ is equal to ${ O(n^{log_b{a}-\epsilon}) }$
    • then ${T(n) = \theta(n^{log_b{a}})}$
  • case 2: if f(n) is equal to ${\Theta(n^{log_b{a}})}$
    • then ${T(n) = \theta(n^{log_b{a}}log{n})}$
  • case 3: if f(n) is equal to ${\Omega(n^{log_b{a}+\epsilon})}$
    • then ${T(n) = O(f(n))}$
