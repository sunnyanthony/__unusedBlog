Type of hypervisor

When I learned about hypervisor is in the virtualization cousre. I thought hypervisor is about full-virtualization and para-virtualization. But I am misunderstanding about that. I throught type1 is full-virtualization and type2 is para-virtualization.
So, I will introduce what are type1 and type2 about hypervisor.

The paper,Formal Requirements for Virtualizable Third Generation Architectures, classified hypervisor to two types. Type1 is bare-metal hypervisors and Tper2 is hypervisors in host OS. The type1 is running on the hardware and donâ€™t need OS help to control hardware. Type2 is dependent the OS to control hardware and it is also using OS service support.

You can consider type-1 hypervisor just is emulation hardware, management VM and support guest OS. Finally, the type of hypervisor can be full or para virtualization, e.g. type-1 para-virtualization Xen VMM.
